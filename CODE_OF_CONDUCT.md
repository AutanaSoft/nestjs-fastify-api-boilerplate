# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on technical merit and collaboration
- Assume positive intent
- Accept feedback gracefully

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and external communication channels related to this project.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to: `github@autanasoft.com`.

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
